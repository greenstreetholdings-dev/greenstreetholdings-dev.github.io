What we do

Five ways to partner.

No two businesses are alike — so we don't force them into one structure. From full acquisition to advisory, we tailor the relationship to what each business actually needs to grow.

01

Full Acquisitions

Majority / 100% ownership

We acquire businesses outright, providing the resources, governance, and expertise to unlock their full potential — while honoring the legacy and people that made them worth acquiring.

  • Founder & succession transitions
  • Operational and strategic turnaround
  • Long-term ownership, not a quick flip
02

Partial Ownership

Significant minority / control stakes

We invest in promising businesses, taking a significant stake and working shoulder-to-shoulder with management to drive sustained, durable growth.

  • Active board & strategic involvement
  • Aligned incentives with founders
  • Capital for expansion & modernization
03

Minority Investments

Growth equity

We provide capital and strategic support to high-growth companies — enabling them to scale, innovate, and reach the next stage without surrendering their independence.

  • Minority stakes with founder control
  • Access to our networks & expertise
  • Support through inflection points
04

Growth Capital

Flexible financing

We provide access to capital and financial resources to fuel your growth — structured flexibly around your goals, not a rigid fund mandate.

  • Expansion, working capital & capex
  • Tailored to cash-flow realities
  • Patient, long-horizon terms
05

Advisory

Strategic guidance

We offer expert advice to help you identify growth opportunities and develop tailored strategies — drawing on deep experience in strategy, data, and capital formation.

  • Strategy, data & analytics
  • Capital formation & readiness
  • Operating & organizational design
How we work

From first conversation to lasting partnership

01

Conversation

We start by listening. We want to understand your business, your goals, and what success looks like to you — before discussing any structure.

02

Diligence

We do our homework rigorously and transparently — bringing data and strategy expertise to a clear-eyed view of the opportunity.

03

Structure

We design the partnership around what the business needs — equity, capital, advisory, or a blend — with terms built for the long term.

04

Partnership & growth

We stay close — contributing expertise, networks, and capital as the business compounds value over the years that follow.
