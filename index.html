Our purpose

We invest where capital is scarce and conviction is rewarded — backing founders across Latin America with the resources, expertise, and patience to compound value over decades, not quarters.

We are a holding company, not a fund on a clock. That distinction shapes everything: how we choose partners, how we structure deals, and how long we stay.

From management consulting to forestry and impact investing, our founders bring rare, complementary expertise to every business we touch.
