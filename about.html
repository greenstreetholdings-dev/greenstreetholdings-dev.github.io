Who we are

Conviction, expertise, and the long view.

Green Street Holdings is a holding company built around a simple belief: that exceptional businesses can transform their communities.

Our mission

We partner with small and mid-sized businesses across Latin America — equipping them with capital, expertise, and resources to achieve transformative growth, and to build enduring, collaborative success.

We stay close to the businesses we back, respect the communities they serve, and understand the region's particular opportunities and constraints.

It's an approach grounded in partnership rather than extraction; in building rather than flipping.

The founders

Founder & Managing Partner

Lucho Escobedo

Lucho is an entrepreneur and strategic leader with over 15 years of experience at the intersection of technology, management consulting, and business innovation. At McKinsey & Company, he honed his ability to solve complex challenges for global organizations — the foundation for a career dedicated to transformative leadership.

He has co-founded several ventures: a data and analytics consulting firm serving clients across Latin America and the Caribbean; a consumer-goods company building premium, authentic brands; and an end-to-end services company supporting entrepreneurs from inception to tax and human-capital management.

  • —Former consultant, McKinsey & Company

  • —Focus on strategy, data & capital formation
Est. 2024
Founder & General Partner

Carol Burga

Carol is Co-founder and General Partner at Green Street Holdings, where she leads impact-investment initiatives and helps shape the firm's vision. A published author and advocate for responsible investment, she fosters partnerships that drive meaningful, measurable change.

With over 15 years empowering Indigenous and local communities in forest nations, Carol is a trusted expert across the Amazon and forestry communities in Southeast Asia and Africa. She holds a Master's in Forest Governance and Indigenous Peoples, and currently facilitates stakeholder engagement for jurisdictional REDD+ programs worldwide.

  • —M.Sc. Forest Governance & Indigenous Peoples
  • —Published author on the Amazon, forest resources & Indigenous communities
  • —Stakeholder engagement, jurisdictional REDD+ programs
What guides us

The principles behind every partnership

01

Partnership over control

We succeed when founders succeed. We structure every relationship around shared incentives and mutual respect.

02

Long-term thinking

We measure success in decades. Long-horizon capital lets businesses make the right decisions, not just the urgent ones.

03

Responsible by design

Impact and returns are not in tension. We back businesses that create value for shareholders, communities, and the environment alike.

04

Operating, not observing

We roll up our sleeves. Our experience in strategy, data, and capital formation is applied directly alongside management.

05

Regional fluency

We understand Latin America from the inside — its markets, its people, and the realities of building a business here.

06

Honest counsel

We tell founders what we believe, not what's easy to hear. Trust is the foundation of every lasting partnership.
